There is a lonely man who lives in a small town. Once he had a family and a good job, but he starts lying. He ends up losing his job, and his family leaves him. Most of the people who were living in that town Don’t trust him. 
One day, a fire starts in his home. He runs out of his home and hurriedly goes to the center of the town and shouts to the other people to get help. But nobody believes him, so they Don’t go to his home to help him. His home burns down completely, and he leaves his hometown.
