Two hundred years ago, there was a princess named May Yee. She wanted to get married to a young man, but he was very poor. Her mother was against the marriage.
One day, a fierce dragon appeared in their town. It killed a lot of the villagers. Then the Queen said whoever killed the dragon would get his wish granted by her. The young man went to kill the dragon. The dragon was very strong, and they fought a great battle. Fortunately, the young man killed the dragon. After that the queen agreed that they could get married.
